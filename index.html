    I'm Renan.

I also worked for a large company in the Tool Shop department where we used to make the molds for the parts in aluminum, magnesium, and plastic for our final products like chainsaws, grass trimmers, and blowers.

In the middle of 2022, I decided to take the coding experience more seriously, I started taking some online courses which made me more passionate about the tech world. In January of 2023, I decide to give a step ahead making this fun experience even more serious, I started the Coding Bootcamp at the University of Toronto, looking for a bright career in the tech world in a near future.
